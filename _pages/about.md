Hello, I'm Ezequiel, a solo game developer from Brazil. Just a one-person army creating games from scratch. 

---

### 🛠️ Special Thanks (Tools I Use)
I am deeply grateful to the entire open-source community. These are the tools that power my projects and my day-to-day work:

### 🎮 Game Dev & Art
* [Godot](https://godotengine.org/) - The heart of development. ⚙️
* [Pixelorama](https://pixelorama.org/) - For beauty and creativity in pixel art. 🎨
* [Blender](https://www.blender.org/) - For fantastic 3D creation and low-poly pixel art. 🗿

### 🎬 Audio & Video
* [LMMS](https://lmms.io/) - A symphony orchestra in your computer. 🎼
* [Audacity](https://www.audacityteam.org/) - The multi-tool for sound design and audio cleanup. 🎤
* [Kdenlive](https://kdenlive.org/) - Cutting and weaving my videos together smoothly. 🎞️
* [OBS Studio](https://obsproject.com/) - Capturing my screen and dev moments effortlessly. 📹
* [VLC Media Player](https://www.videolan.org/) - The undisputed king: it just plays everything. 🧡

### 👨🏻‍💻 Code & Documentation
* [Git](https://git-scm.com/) & [GitHub](https://github.com/) - My personal time machine. ⏳
* [Geany](https://www.geany.org/) - Fast as a text editor, powerful as an IDE. 🚀
* [LibreOffice](https://www.libreoffice.org/) - My trusty suite for all things documentation. 📝

### 🐧 System & Utilities
* [Linux Mint (Cinnamon)](https://www.linuxmint.com/) - My main workstation: Fast, lightweight, stable, and a real development powerhouse. 🌿
* [EndeavourOS (Xfce)](https://endeavouros.com/) - My secondary laptop: Minimalist, bleeding-edge, and my go-to setup for coding on the move. 💻
* [Syncthing](https://syncthing.net/) - Keeping my project files updated in real-time across devices. 🔄
* [Firefox](https://www.firefox.com/) - Wait, isn't that a fox?! 🦊
* [7-Zip](https://7-zip.org) - Unpack any file instantly. 📦

Thank you to the developers behind these projects!
